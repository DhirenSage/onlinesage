Chancellor’s Corner

Er. Sanjeev Agrawal

Chancellor, SAGE University Indore & Bhopal

Chancellor’s Corner

“My mission is nation-building through education and beyond.”

At SAGE University, Indore, a NAAC A+ accredited institution and the youngest university in India to earn this distinction, we are committed to transforming passion into profession. Our focus is on delivering academic excellence through an industry-aligned curriculum, expert faculty, and personalized mentorship, ensuring that every student develops both competence and character.

SAGE University Indore serves as a launchpad for ambitious learners, providing an environment where curiosity is nurtured and excellence is achieved. Beyond academics, we strive to shape ethical, responsible, and visionary leaders who contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

As a temple of learning, our university empowers students through diverse programs, advanced technologies, and multidisciplinary exposure, affirming that knowledge is the foundation for limitless opportunities. Positioned at a defining stage in a student’s journey, SAGE University Indore guides them toward realizing their full potential and building a brighter future.

As Chancellor, I invite students and parents to be part of this transformative journey. Our vision is to establish SAGE University as a global benchmark in higher education and industry collaboration. Together, let us embrace the philosophy: “SAGE Hai To Mumkin Hai” - Where there is SAGE, there is possibility.
