Program Overview

SAGE's specialized online MBA in Banking and Finance develops expertise in financial services, banking operations, investment strategies, and corporate finance. This program combines financial theory with practical applications in today's dynamic financial markets.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2

Program Highlights

Focused banking and finance specialization

Coverage of investments and risk management

Market-aligned curriculum for financial sectors

Financial modeling and analytical techniques

Flexible online delivery with expert faculty
