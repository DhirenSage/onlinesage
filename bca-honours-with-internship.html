Program Overview

SAGE's BCA Honours with Research (Degree with Internship) uniquely blends computing education with real industry immersion. Students gain technical expertise through online academics while simultaneously building professional experience through structured IT industry internships.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2

Program Highlights

Solid grounding in programming and software fundamentals

Real IT industry internship throughout program

Emerging tech coverage: AI, Machine Learning, Data Science

Current industry-aligned content

Flexible online delivery with recorded and live sessions

Specialized electives in advanced domains
