The Leadership

Er. Sanjeev Agrawal

Chancellor, SAGE University Indore & Bhopal
CMD, The SAGE Group

“My mission is nation-building through education and beyond.”

Education is the most sacred pursuit, and at The SAGE Group, we have been dedicated to this noble cause since our inception in 1983. Over the past 37 years, we have built a legacy of excellence rooted in quality education, innovation, and integrity.

At SAGE, we believe in nurturing students not just as professionals, but as responsible citizens ready to contribute to the nation’s progress. Our goal is to provide world-class education through a dynamic, learner-centric environment—equipped with advanced infrastructure, cutting-edge laboratories, and a distinguished faculty.

We focus on holistic development, ensuring our students acquire life skills, values, and the confidence to face the challenges of a fast-changing world. Beyond academic success, we aim to create skilled, smart, and socially conscious individuals who drive positive transformation in society and beyond.

Ms. Sakshi Bansal

Managing Director, The SAGE Group

“Dreams of doing great things reflect the enthusiasm of a student.”

Dear Students,

Welcome to SAGE University, Indore—a place where innovation meets inspiration. On behalf of the entire SAGE community, I invite you to begin a new era of learning that blends knowledge, creativity, and purpose.

At SUI, we are shaping the future by integrating educational excellence with innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology. Our mission is to nurture talent, instill leadership qualities, and empower students to thrive in a rapidly evolving world. Through experiential learning and a dynamic academic framework, we encourage students to think critically, adapt quickly, and lead confidently.

We believe that true education lies in developing resilience and responsiveness—the traits that define future-ready professionals. I wish each one of you a meaningful, productive, and inspiring academic journey ahead.

Warm regards,
Ms. Sakshi Bansal
Managing Director

Dr. Prashant Jain

Pro-Chancellor, SAGE University Indore

“Let’s transform your career into a profession.”

Dear Students,

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to SAGE University, Indore, a vision realized under the guidance of our Founder and Chancellor, Er. Sanjeev Agrawal. Spread over 25 acres of serene green landscape, the University is designed to offer a world-class educational experience that bridges knowledge with opportunity.

SAGE University combines modern infrastructure, a vibrant learning environment, and a wide spectrum of multidisciplinary programs—from Engineering and Management to Applied Sciences, Humanities, Design, Agriculture, and beyond. Our approach ensures that students develop a balanced mix of academic excellence and practical expertise.

In a world of constant change, we encourage students to explore, question, and innovate. We take pride in producing well-disciplined, value-driven professionals who uphold the highest standards of competence and ethics.

SAGE’s vision is to cultivate tomorrow’s entrepreneurs, scientists, engineers, and leaders—individuals who can drive innovation and contribute meaningfully to global progress. As you embark on this new journey, I encourage you to remain focused, committed, and passionate in pursuit of your dreams.

Wishing you a fulfilling and successful academic session.
Warm regards,
Dr. Prashant Jain
Pro-Chancellor

Prof. Dr. Ankur Arun Kulkarni

Vice-Chancellor, SAGE University Indore

Dear Students and Parents,

It is an honor to serve as the Vice-Chancellor of SAGE University, Indore, and to welcome learners from across the country to our academic community.

Education is one of the most powerful catalysts for national growth. At SAGE, our goal is to transform young minds into capable thinkers, innovators, and leaders who can contribute to India’s development and global competitiveness.

Nestled in a scenic campus, SAGE University offers a student-centric learning ecosystem backed by a strong academic framework, dedicated faculty, and vibrant co-curricular opportunities. Through project-based learning, research initiatives, and national and international collaborations, we aim to instill critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills among students.

Our strategic vision rests on four pillars: Excellence in Teaching, Student Satisfaction, Research and Innovation, and Social Responsibility. We are committed to fostering flexibility, community partnership, and fearlessness in experimentation—qualities that will define the leaders of tomorrow.

Welcome to SAGE University, where learning inspires change and knowledge creates possibilities.

Sincerely,
Prof. Dr. Ankur Arun Kulkarni
Vice-Chancellor

Dr. Ankur Saxena

Pro Vice-Chancellor, SAGE University Indore

Dear Students and Faculty,

SAGE University continues the proud legacy of The SAGE Group in shaping professionals for global careers. As one of India’s leading private universities, SAGE represents a unique fusion of technology, innovation, and creativity—aimed at preparing graduates who make a meaningful impact in business and society.

Our programs span a wide range of disciplines, including Applied Sciences, Agriculture, Computing, Engineering, Business, Finance, and Humanities, supported by an academic model that promotes innovation, continuous improvement, and outcome-based learning.

At SAGE, technology acts as the common thread across all programs, ensuring that students gain relevant, future-ready skills. Our Project-Based Learning approach emphasizes hands-on experience from day one, bridging theory with practice and fostering a mindset of innovation.

Backed by a legacy of national and international recognition, SAGE University stands as a hub of excellence—committed to nurturing talent, advancing research, and building professionals who are globally competent and socially responsible.

Warm regards,
Dr. Ankur Saxena
