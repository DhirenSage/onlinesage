Program Overview

SAGE's MCA with Internship integrates advanced computing education with professional IT workplace experience. Students master software engineering, advanced technologies, and system design while contributing to real industry projects through partnerships.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2
